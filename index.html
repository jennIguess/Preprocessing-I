I'm the boy who lived

I grew up an unwelcome orphan, living with my aunt and uncle in Little Whinging. One day just before my eleventh birthday, an owl tried to deliver a mysterious letter—the first of a sequence of events that resulted in meeting a giant man named Hagrid. Hagrid explained my history to me: When I was a baby, the Dark wizard, Lord Voldemort, attacked and killed my parents in an attempt to kill me; because I alone had the power to extinguish The Dark Lord. This illustrates that I've literally been vanquishing evil since I was born. I'm pretty much a natural.

Here's what I can do
  • Expecto patronum – to summon up a Patronus.
  • Accio – to call an object to you.
  • Wingardium Leviosa – to levitate objects.
  • Expelliarmus –to disarm your opponent.
  • Lumos – to ignite one's wand-tip to provide light.
  • Alohomora – to open locked windows and doors.
  • Avada Kedavra – to bring about instant death upon one's opponent.
